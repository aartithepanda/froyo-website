Learn more about us!

FroYo XPress is more than just frozen yogurt. We leverage the fundamental concept of autonomously dispensing food and beverages to increase affordability and accessibility of food. Digitization of all business is coming and we are on the forefront of this trend. Large companies will not be the only ones to benefit. Our mission is to keep small and mid-sized food and beverage vendors savvy as digitization becomes transformative to their space. It doesn't hurt that everyone loves froyo. By automating retail food and beverage dispensing, FroYo XPress will make nourishment cheaper and more accessible to current consumers, and in the long term, will allow lower income consumers to have access to food that they never had access to before. Technology brings retail infrastructure to a turning point, as our ideas of automation finally have the resources to be brought to market. Our mission is to engage food and beverage retailers with the future of digitized business, while making food and beverage highly accessible and more affordable to consumers of all kinds.
